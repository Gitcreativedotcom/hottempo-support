🎵 HotTempo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

HotTempo ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use HotTempo:

  • Spotify Data: Your top artists, top tracks, listening history, and music preferences — used solely to power music compatibility matching.
  • Profile Information: Your name, age, bio, photos, and location that you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: App interactions such as swipes, matches, and messages to improve the service.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens to send you match and message alerts.

How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with other users based on music compatibility
  • To display your profile to potential matches
  • To facilitate messaging between matched users
  • To send push notifications about matches and messages
  • To show you upcoming concerts based on your top artists
  • To improve and personalize your experience

Spotify Data

HotTempo connects to Spotify via OAuth. We access your listening data only to calculate music compatibility scores. We do not store your Spotify password. We do not sell or share your Spotify data with any third parties. You can disconnect Spotify at any time by revoking access in your Spotify account settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider for storing user data securely.
  • Ticketmaster / Eventbrite: To fetch concert event data based on your top artists.
  • Giphy: To power the GIF feature in chat.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Voice notes are automatically deleted after 3 days. You may delete your account at any time from the app settings, which permanently removes all your data from our systems.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for Spotify data access at any time

Children's Privacy

HotTempo is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including row-level security on our database, encrypted data transmission via HTTPS, and secure OAuth authentication. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
