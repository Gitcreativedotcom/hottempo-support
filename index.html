Frequently Asked Questions

How does music matching work?

HotTempo connects to your Spotify account and analyzes your top artists, tracks and genres to calculate a compatibility score with other users.

Why can't I see any profiles?

Make sure your Spotify is connected and your profile is complete. Try closing and reopening the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → HotTempo → Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

Why is my Spotify not connecting?

Log out of HotTempo completely, reopen the app and try connecting Spotify again.

Privacy Policy

HotTempo only uses your Spotify data for music matching. We never sell your personal data.
